Measured UX is a full service creative agency that specializes in websites for small businesses. Combining the specialties of user experience design with visual design, we communicate brand and story through digital sites and experiences. 

 
 

The Team

 

Teague Nelson

Teague graduated from Randolph College in Lynchburg, Va and continued his education at the University of Florida's Historic Preservation Institute on Nantucket Island. Professionally, Teague has worked as a designer and developer with creative agencies in Seattle and New York City. Some of the clients Teague has worked with are Starbucks, Columbia University, ReMain Nantucket, and Jacob's Pillow Dance Institute.

Teague is interested in how people understand things through the use of story and uses this as a design model to create cohesive and engaging digital experiences.

 

 

Margo Nelson

Margo graduated from the Savannah College of Art and Design in Savannah, GA. She has worked as a designer for Martha Stewart Living as well as Tommy Hilfiger. Originally from the Pacific Northwest, Margo now lives in New York City.

Margo has a passion is for telling unique stories through graphics, color and typography. Her visual design talent allows her to translate stories into digital storefronts that are a brand's first introduction to the public.

Some personal interests include hand illustration, travel, and playing with her friend’s rambunctious miniature Labridoodle.
